In-Person Sessions

Public and stakeholder consultation sessions were held in thirteen locations: Toronto (2), Oshawa, Mississauga, Thunder Bay, Sudbury, Kingston, Ottawa, London, Aurora, Windsor, Guelph, Hamilton and Kitchener. The stakeholder sessions allowed for a range of groups to be represented including various sectors, including industry (petrochemical, biochemical, food, steel, cement and automobile industries), as well as municipalities, conservation authorities, the Ontario Farming Association, academia, health sector, energy sector, and non-government.
